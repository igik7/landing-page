Become a Pirate Legend!

Pirate Legend is a milestone Title that, once earned, grants players additional perks within Sea of Thieves.
The title is awarded to players that have reached level 50 in three different Trading Companies, and bought the corresponding level 50 promotions.
Players must then speak to the Mysterious Stranger, who will reward the Title, the Legendary Set clothing, and the Shanty of Legends that's used to access the Athena's Fortune Hideout.
