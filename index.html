Become a Pirate Legend!

Pirate Legend is a milestone Title that, once earned, grants players additional perks within Sea of Thieves.
The title is awarded to players that have reached level 50 in three different Trading Companies,
and bought the corresponding level 50 promotions.

Here are some of the Pirate Legend status perks...
Title and Clothes
Athena's Fortune Hideout
Legendary Mercenary Voyages
Legendary Pet Outfits
